SUPPORT FOR FOSTER CARE KIDS(SFFCK)

What We Do?

Description

We are a small group located in Bruce, Mississippi. We have employed over 100 people to help our cause to help kids in need of a good home. We take charities and our earnings and donate it to the group "Foster Kids In Need". We have helped over 2,000 people so far and we want you to help too. We help a wide variety of people and try and give foster kids a place to stay whether in our hands or a family. We prevent child-abuse, and support permanent guardianship.

Goals

  • Help Kids In Need
  • Give Kids a Home
  • Give Them a Loving Family
